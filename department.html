Departments Available In GPI

We Offers Three Years Of Diploma In IT, Electrical and Civil Technology

Civil Department

The Civil Engineering Department focuses on building strong foundations for the future. Students learn about construction, surveying, structural design, and modern engineering techniques. The department aims to prepare skilled engineers who can design, develop, and maintain sustainable infrastructure for society.

Computer Information Technology

An IT department manages and maintains an organization's technology, including its hardware, software, and networks, to ensure efficient, secure, and seamless operations. Its responsibilities range from providing user support and data security to strategic planning for technology infrastructure and ensuring compliance with IT governance. The department's specific functions can include installing and updating software, managing company websites, and troubleshooting technical issues for employees.

Electrical Department

An electrical department is a unit responsible for the design, installation, maintenance, and operation of electrical systems and equipment. Its functions vary depending on the context, from overseeing building-wide electrical systems in construction to managing power, lighting, and locomotives in a railway. In an academic setting, an electrical engineering department focuses on educating students in the principles of electrical engineering through coursework and hands-on lab work, preparing them for careers in various subfields.
